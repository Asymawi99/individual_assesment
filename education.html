MY EDUCATION JOURNEY

For this page, I will explain my education journey from I was in primary school until today, continuing my Bachelor of Finance.

PRIMARY SCHOOL

My childhood learning started at Sekolah Kebangsaan Kor. The school is placed near my house and it only take 10 minute if i walk to school. It is a rural primary school located in my village. So mostly of the students are from the same neighbourhood. I manage to finish my primary learning at the school and get 5A for the UPSR and it is still a shocked to me as i was not expecting for the result to be good.


HIGH SCHOOL

After finish primary school, i continue my study to Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Mulong for my secondary learning. At there i meet new friends and make memories as i become a teenager. I'm a passive student and do not have any title during my high school day. I also do not participate in an school event as i don't like to be in public. For SPM i only manage to get 5A which is quite good for me based on my hardwork.


DIPLOMA AND DEGREE

Next, after successfully finished my study at the high school level, I have continued to further my study by taking a Diploma in Accountancy at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang. During my study in UiTM Machang, I have make many memories with my new friends at there. At the university i do not belong to any club as i'm not a diligent person to do another extra work for me. In addition, i manage to finish my diploma after three years of study. And right now i'm continuing my study at UiTM Kota Bharu for my Bachelor Degree.
